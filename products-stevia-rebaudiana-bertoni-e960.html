PRODUCTS - STEVIA

HISTORY & ORIGIN

Stevia is a plant-based sweetener that has been used for centuries in South America to sweeten drinks and food. The cultivation areas have their origin in South America. Traditionally, they still exist, but the main cultivation areas have shifted to Asia due to industrialization.

ADVANTAGES

Calorie-free, 100% natural sweetener, suitable for diabetics, can contribute to weight loss, high sweetening power, particularly tooth-friendly, heat stability up to 200 degrees.
